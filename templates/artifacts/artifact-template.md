# Purpose

This document defines the standard structure for every artifact generated by the EditorialAfrica platform.

Artifacts are permanent outputs produced by AI agents.

Artifacts are:

- versioned
- traceable
- reproducible
- auditable

Every generated artifact SHALL conform to this specification.

---
