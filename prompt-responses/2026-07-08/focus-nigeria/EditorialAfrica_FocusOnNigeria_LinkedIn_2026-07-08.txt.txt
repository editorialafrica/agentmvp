In today’s Wednesday edition of #FocusOnNigeria, we look at the heavy institutional fatigue gripping the nation across both the legislative and executive spheres. 

Key takeaways from our media analysis:
🔹 The Gbajagate Scandal: Punch Newspapers demands the National Assembly use its power of the purse to strike the N1.3 billion "PFIPC" allocation from the supplementary budget and launch an immediate public probe.

The structural deficits are clear, and the press is demanding immediate action over rhetoric.
